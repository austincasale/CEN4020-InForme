About Us

InforMe was developed by a small team of four learning programmers! We all study at Florida State University, and have each used InforMe to take a deep dive into programming languages we have never used before. Our goal is simple: create a minimalistic web interface that allows users to summarize articles on-the-fly, and then quickly share those article summaries with their friends. It makes digesting the news quick, easy, and ad-free.
